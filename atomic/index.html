What It Does

Simple workflow for tracking mod changes.

Snapshot

Save your current load order as "Known Good" with one click.

Compare

See exactly what changed - added, removed, or reordered mods.

Report

Generate clean Markdown reports ready to paste to Discord/Reddit/Nexus.

Private

Runs 100% offline. Your mod data never leaves your computer.

What It Isn't

Atomic Diff is...

  • A load order snapshot tool
  • A diff/comparison viewer
  • A help report generator
  • Free and open source

It's NOT...

  • A crash fixer or debugger
  • A load order optimizer
  • A LOOT replacement
  • A mod manager
