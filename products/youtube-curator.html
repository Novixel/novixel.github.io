What niches work best?

Motivational, educational, news recap, gaming highlights, and lifestyle content tend to perform well. We'll discuss options during consultation.

The AI curates and transforms content legally under fair use principles, with proper attribution. We also integrate royalty-free and original content generation.

What are the ongoing costs?

After setup, you'll need hosting for the automation (~$20-50/mo) and optional AI API costs (~$10-30/mo). No revenue sharing, ever.

How long until monetization?

YouTube requires 1,000 subscribers and 10M Shorts views for monetization. Timeline varies, but consistent posting typically achieves this in 3-6 months.
