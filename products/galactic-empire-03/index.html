But the ore keeps breathing into it.

I didn't believe it at first. The day I connected the first chunk of that humming mineral to the auxiliary power coupling, I expected nothing. Maybe a spark. Maybe another small explosion to add to my collection of scars. Instead, a light flickered. Dim. Brief. But real...
