The Story

Three years stranded on a planet that wants her dead. Ezrae survives on alien ore and stubborn rage, slowly rebuilding a ship from wreckage that should have been her tomb.

When she finally breaks the sky, she discovers a galaxy shattered by war—and a dimensional Rift that's slowly consuming everything that's left. Pirates, factions, alien races, and ancient technology collide as she rises from survivor to leader, from prey to predator.

They call her the Pirate Queen. She never asked for that name. But as the Rift stirs and the darkness returns, she may be the only one who can unite the scattered survivors against an enemy that wants to devour them all.
